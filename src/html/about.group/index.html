About
Seven Castles Ranch

7 Castles Ranch is a breathtaking property set on the western slope of the Continental Divide on the Frying Pan River. Five miles above Basalt, Colorado on Frying Pan road, 7 Castles Ranch offers spectacular western daylight views and star filled nights with the expansive White River National Forest literally at your doorstep. Genuine ranch adventures, world-class private fishing, on your own ranch river water, wilderness hiking in your backyard and much more awaits you.

A completely remodeled classic Aspen “A frame” that has a spacious main floor living room, together with over 1,000 square feet of newly added decking space to enjoy the incredible views at 7 Castles Ranch. Downstairs, a full lounge, sauna, wet/dry bar, and hot tub assures you plenty of space for family and friends to spread out and enjoy each other and the Ranch after a full day of adventure.
