The
Farm House

A beautiful estate set in the Berkshire Mountains in upstate New York

This charming farmhouse is as ideal for corporate retreats as well as family gatherings. Find yourself exploring the over 300 acres, take a summer's night dip in the beautiful clear pond or an afternoon sauna after a day of skiing at nearby Jiminy Peak. This property has it all! Sleeps 14 with 5 Large Bedrooms, 4 Full Baths & 3 Half Baths. Dining areas to seat more than 35, Butlers Pantry, Chef's Kitchen and several Living Spaces. Groups with children will love the loft bedroom with 6 plush bunk beds, projector & screen to watch your favorite movies!

Three Wishes Barn

Need more space? Three Wishes Barn is a freestanding remodeled barn with a honeymoon suite style loft bedroom and vaulted ceiling. The balcony views and clawfoot tub create a calming space with main floor living as well as a canning kitchen. Perfect space for catering, bridal parties or simply a cozy getaway. The
Geography

Location Is Everything

Tucked away in the foothills of the Berkshires, James and Hudson Farm allows guests to take advantage of everything the region has to offer. While visiting you’ll have access to over 150 acres of picturesque countryside to explore, numerous critically acclaimed museums such as MASS MoCA and Norman Rockwell Museum and all the historical sites sprinkled throughout the area. The Berkshires truly offer something for everyone and James and Hudson farm is the perfect place to make lasting memories.

Make Your Special Occasion Memorable

James and Hudson Farm is a premier events location and quaint get-away nestled in upstate New York. The Farm is set in the beautiful Berkshire Mountains, providing plenty of space for gathering of all sizes. The property comfortable sleeps sixteen making it the perfect spot for family reunions, corporate retreats, wedding parties, or whatever your group can imagine. While staying at James and Hudson farm you’ll find yourself surrounded by luxury amenities such as a private sauna, massive chefs’ kitchen, several enclosed and open-air wrap-around porches and so much more!
